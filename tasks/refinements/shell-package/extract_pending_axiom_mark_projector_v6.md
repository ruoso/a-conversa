## Why it needs to be done

The v5 audit closed with the explicit pre-registration of THIS `_v6` task at unchanged trigger semantics (both UI leaves `complete 100`), AND with the explicit obligation that `_v6` complete the cadence-revisit-evidence window the v3 precedent named (v5 Decision §3 alternative A's chosen path: hold the rate at `_v5`, scope the rate change at `_v6` if findings remain identical). Both are the refinement-stage activity this leaf delivers.

Four observations make the audit valuable (rather than a no-op formality at one further iteration's distance from the originating v1):

1. **The v5 audit identified the same revision path the v1, v2, v3, and v4 audits named (the participant and audience refinements would have to explicitly revise their principled-absence decisions), and one further audit-iteration is the right place to re-check whether that path has been walked.** A search of the two work-streams' refinements + their `.tji` task blocks at `_v6` commit time shows zero revisions: [`part_axiom_mark_decoration.md`](../participant-ui/part_axiom_mark_decoration.md) Status line says "Done — 2026-05-17" with no amendments; [`aud_axiom_mark_decoration.md`](../audience/aud_axiom_mark_decoration.md) Status line says "Done — 2026-05-28" with no amendments. No new participant leaf scoping graph-canvas pending-axiom-mark rendering; no new audience leaf scoping `aud_axiom_mark_pending_render` or any analog; no commits touching either surface's stance on pending-axiom-marks (no commits at all between `b38afc2` and this `_v6` audit). The principled-absence is durable across six audit iterations, not transient.
2. **The sibling `extract_votes_by_facet_projector_v2` lift remains the load-bearing asymmetry citation.** The v2 + v3 + v4 + v5 audits' Decision §1 each recorded in full why that `_v2` lifted (in-flight methodology task closed the divergence half; two byte-identical projector bodies across two existing callers gave evidence weight to relaxing the third-workspace half) and why this audit chain's pending-axiom-mark `_v2` / `_v3` / `_v4` / `_v5` did NOT (the methodology is settled in the principled-absence direction; the trigger is genuinely binary on UI work that is not scheduled). The asymmetry is unchanged at `_v6` audit time and continues to be the durable reason this chain's `_vN` continues to defer while the votes-by-facet `_v2` terminated by lifting. The audit records this so a future reader sees at one glance why the two chains diverged at this disposition step without having to re-derive the methodology context from the source refinements.
3. **Closing this `_v6` leaf cleanly with a cadence-rate change prevents pick-task queue churn at a smaller marginal rate going forward.** Leaving the leaf open on the orchestrator's queue without a fresh audit means every pick-task pass either re-refines it (refinement-document churn for no new content) or skips it ambiguously. The closer marks the leaf `complete 100`; the future-trigger work is held by a freshly-named `_v7` follow-up (Decision §4) whose lift trigger is unchanged but whose **pickup cadence shifts from per-pass to batched** (every N pick-passes since `_v6` close, OR a precondition-touching commit, whichever comes first). The pick-task queue retains a citable home for the deferral while consuming proportionally less orchestrator attention per unit of evidence.
4. **The cadence-revisit-evidence window the v3 precedent named is now complete; the evidence supports a cadence-rate change for `_v7`.** v3 Decision §4 set the precedent that the cadence-rate question may be re-asked at `_v5` / `_v6` — across a two-iteration evidence window, NOT at any single iteration. v4 Decision §3 held the rate while reaching the precedent forward. v5 Decision §3 acknowledged the revisit-question as now-active but held the rate pending the second-iteration confirmation. `_v6` is that second iteration. Two consecutive audit-iterations with bit-for-bit identical findings (both surfacing zero participant code, zero audience code, zero scheduled UI leaves, zero refinement-status amendments, zero precondition-touching commits across each interval) is the evidence the precedent was designed to gather. Acting on the revisit at `_v6` honors the precedent's two-iteration window; deferring further would short-circuit it in the opposite direction (treating the precedent as if it required three or four iterations rather than two). Decision §3 records the cadence-rate decision; Decision §4 bakes the new cadence into `_v7`.

- **Prevents premature ossification.** The three-caller policy's whole point is to wait for empirical convergence rather than to design-shape-and-hope.
