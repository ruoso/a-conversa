## Why it needs to be done

The v4 audit closed with the explicit pre-registration of THIS `_v5` task at unchanged trigger semantics (both UI leaves `complete 100`). The orchestrator's pick-task pass surfaced this task on cadence. The pass cannot verify whether the participant + audience leaves have materialized between v4's commit (`75c784a`, 2026-05-29) and this `_v5`'s commit — that is the refinement-stage activity this leaf delivers.

Four observations make the audit valuable (rather than a no-op formality at one further iteration's distance from the originating v1):

1. **The v4 audit identified the same revision path the v1, v2, and v3 audits named (the participant and audience refinements would have to explicitly revise their principled-absence decisions), and one further audit-iteration is the right place to re-check whether that path has been walked.** A search of the two work-streams' refinements + their `.tji` task blocks at `_v5` commit time shows zero revisions: [`part_axiom_mark_decoration.md`](../participant-ui/part_axiom_mark_decoration.md) Status line says "Done — 2026-05-17" with no amendments; [`aud_axiom_mark_decoration.md`](../audience/aud_axiom_mark_decoration.md) Status line says "Done — 2026-05-28" with no amendments. No new participant leaf scoping graph-canvas pending-axiom-mark rendering; no new audience leaf scoping `aud_axiom_mark_pending_render` or any analog; no commits touching either surface's stance on pending-axiom-marks (no commits at all between `75c784a` and this `_v5` audit). The principled-absence is durable across five audit iterations, not transient.
2. **The sibling `extract_votes_by_facet_projector_v2` lift remains the load-bearing asymmetry citation.** The v2 + v3 + v4 audits' Decision §1 each recorded in full why that `_v2` lifted (in-flight methodology task closed the divergence half; two byte-identical projector bodies across two existing callers gave evidence weight to relaxing the third-workspace half) and why this audit chain's pending-axiom-mark `_v2` / `_v3` / `_v4` / `_v5` did NOT (the methodology is settled in the principled-absence direction; the trigger is genuinely binary on UI work that is not scheduled). The asymmetry is unchanged at `_v5` audit time and continues to be the durable reason this chain's `_vN` continues to defer while the votes-by-facet `_v2` terminated by lifting. The audit records this so a future reader sees at one glance why the two chains diverged at this disposition step without having to re-derive the methodology context from the source refinements.
3. **Closing this `_v5` leaf cleanly prevents pick-task queue churn at the audit cadence.** Leaving the leaf open on the orchestrator's queue without a fresh audit means every pick-task pass either re-refines it (refinement-document churn for no new content) or skips it ambiguously. The closer marks the leaf `complete 100`; the future-trigger work is held by a freshly-named `_v6` follow-up (Decision §4) whose trigger semantics are identical to `_v5`'s. If the trigger fires before the next audit cadence, the implementer reads `_v6` directly; if not, the next audit refines `_v6` the same way this audit refines `_v5`.
4. **The five-iteration audit chain reaches the v3-named cadence-revisit-question iteration — but `_v5` is still not the right place to ACT on the revisit (only to acknowledge it).** v1 + v2 closed same-day (2026-05-28); v3 + v4 closed same-day (2026-05-29); `_v5` extends the chain to a third orchestrator-pick wave. The v3 precedent (held verbatim by v4 Decision §3) named `_v5` / `_v6` as the iterations at which the cadence-rate revisit becomes appropriate. `_v5` is the FIRST such iteration — the revisit question is now on the table. But the threshold for acting on it is two identical-findings closures across the cadence-revisit window: `_v5` AND `_v6` both closing with bit-for-bit identical audit findings would give a future `_v7` the evidence weight to scope a cadence-rate change. `_v5` alone is half the evidence; acting unilaterally here would short-circuit the precedent v3 set and v4 held. Decision §3 records the cadence-revisit question as now-active-but-held; the rate stays at one-per-orchestrator-pick-pass; the `_v6` audit will be the second half of the cadence-revisit-evidence window.

- **Prevents premature ossification.** The three-caller policy's whole point is to wait for empirical convergence rather than to design-shape-and-hope. Lifting `projectPendingAxiomMarks` into the shell today as a single-caller move "in case" the second and third callers eventually materialize would create a shell-side projector whose only consumer is the moderator's `GraphCanvasPane` — exactly the anti-pattern `extract_cytoscape_projectors` Decision §1 rejected for the participant boolean+count helpers and that `extract_facet_pill` Decision §2 set the three-caller policy to prevent. The v1, v2, v3, and v4 audits rejected this for the same reasons; the `_v5` re-audit confirms the reasoning still holds.
- **Records the named-future-task path crisply at the audit-iteration cadence.** The orchestrator brief's tech-debt registration policy asks every deferred task to be named with effort + description; Decision §4 supplies both for the `_v6` follow-up, freshly stating the trigger semantics one audit-iteration further out.
- **Pins the recipe in advance.** When the trigger does fire (likely 2-4 quarters out, after the participant's pending-proposals pane expands or the audience's broadcast-feedback iteration surfaces a need), the implementer of the terminating-lift refinement reads the audit chain and follows the `shell_axiom_marks_extraction` + `extract_cytoscape_projectors` + `extract_votes_by_facet_projector_v2` recipe verbatim — no design re-derivation needed across the chain.
- **Holds the cadence-revisit precedent open at its named iteration.** The v3-set precedent (revisit at `_v5` / `_v6`, not earlier) was held by `_v4` and is now reached at `_v5`. Acknowledging-without-acting at `_v5` preserves the precedent's two-iteration evidence window cleanly; the `_v6` audit becomes the second half of that window, and a future `_v7` (if the trigger has not fired) is the iteration where the cadence-rate change may actually be scoped.
